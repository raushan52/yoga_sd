about sida yoga

certified

As the owner of sida yoga, Victoria is a Registered Yoga Teacher (RYT200) with Yoga Alliance. Based in Dorset, she mainly teaches Restorative, Alignment and Vinyasa classes using yoga props to allow safe progression and deeper exploration of poses.

experienced

Victoria has travelled all over the world practicing yoga for the nearly 10 years. She is now based in Dorset, England, teaching individuals and groups. Victoria is able to offer a refreshing and energetic style of teaching focusing on the ‘feel good’ factor yoga provides.

individual

Victoria works with her students closely to determine each individual need and develop tailored practices to suit all requirements. Keen to make yoga accessible for everyone, Victoria’s classes are multi-level from beginner to advanced.

benefits of sida yoga

flexibility

Frequently stretching and strengthening the body, stiffness in the joints & muscles are reduced. By connecting deeply to muscle tissue the body becomes more supple.

health

Participating in regular practice has been proven to deliver many health benefits both mental and physical and can often encourage a healthier lifestyle.

relax

Yoga encourages us to slow down. Deep breathing exercises increase the flow of blood and oxygen to each part of the body and tension is released.

balance

By grounding the hands and feet correctly in postures, coordinating movement and using focus, the body becomes stronger and more stable.

energy

Yoga can rejuvenate all the systems within the body, boost energy levels and reduce fatigue.

de-stress

By becoming more in tune with the body the nervous system becomes calmer and stress hormone levels are lowered.

focus

Using controlled breathing techniques and meditative exercises distracting thoughts are put to the back of your mind allowing you to become more present in the moment.

awareness

With good alignment comes increased awareness of what the body is doing and where the body is in space. Senses become clearer and we become more aware of others and our surroundings.
